ABOUT US

Moon Star Agriculture Farm in Pakistan is the first time concept of handling corporate farming through our own infra-structure & human resource on an initial area of 2,000 hectares. We are innovative to conduct modern crop rotations & crop production technologies through mechanization to improve the efficiency of input & handling the harvest to export to international market. Moon Star Agriculture Farm was established in 2009. Our goal is to make quality products. We are team of professionals and hard working businessmen. We have expertise in producing healthy goods. Our products include Chili, Potatoes, Moringa and Peas.

Sultan Mehmood


We believe that the crop rotation is the profitability earned through wise selection of crops, fruits & vegetables after studying market demand & trends of taste. We are eager to improve the mechanization of our farms by sorting the hybrid & multi-tasking machineries for cash crops, vegetables under tunnel farming, fodder to make hay & silage. Our farm are located in farmer's community & designated as Research & Production farms for seed & valuable produce. These farms are to advocate farming community of Pakistan by opening our gates for farmer because "seeing is believing" for adoption of new technologies.
